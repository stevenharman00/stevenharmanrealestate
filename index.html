About me

I'm an independent real estate agent under Coldwell Banker. That, in and of itself probably doesn't sound too impressive or showstopping, as you most likely know several agents already; maybe your kid's schoolteacher is an agent or the Uber driver that took you to happy hour was an agent. But just like you wouldn't take investment advice from those people you probably would prefer someone that is channeled into real estate full time. That's where I come in. My original plan was to participate in real estate part-time to give me access as an investor. Little did I know that bastard of a virus would force me to lose my job as a commercial helicopter pilot. That's what I did before this bounty of nothingness appeared in the form of a commission only job in real estate. I flew tours around Oahu. Before that I flew medical evacuation for hospitals in Arizona and before that, I was an Army helicopter pilot. Oh the hats I have worn to be here in front of you on a web page that I'm coding. This Covid mess has highlighted the need for new skills, so why not add "web developer in training" to the list.
